Cucumber Raita

Simple Cucumber Raita made with yogurt, fresh cucumber, spices and herbs!

It's the perfect accompaniment to Indian meals!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 medium cucumber
  • 1/4 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon kala namak
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped mint

Steps:

  1. Before you start, peel the cucumber and grate it using a grater.
  2. add plain milk yogurt in a bowl and whish it until smooth. now add, chaat masala, kala namak, cumin powder and salt. Mix until spices are well combined with the yogurt.
  3. Then add the grated cucumber to the yogurt and mix.
  4. Stir in the chopped mint leaves and mix.
  5. Garnish with mint and serve cucumber raita as a side with any Indian meal or with biryanis and pulao. .
  6. You may sprinkle some cumin powder on top before serving. I also sprinkle some kashmiri red chili powder for presentation
